My Top 10 Travel Destinations

Palawan

Palawan stuns with the Puerto Princesa Underground River, island hopping in El Nido, and shipwreck diving in Coron.

— Times of India

Cebu

Cebu is famed for its whale shark diving in Oslob, sardine runs in Moalboal, and countryside of Bohol’s Chocolate Hills.

— Mad Monkey Hostels

Seoul

Seoul blends tradition and trend at Namsan Tower views, buzzing K-pop districts, and vibrant night markets.

— Global Traveler USA

Tokyo

Tokyo electrifies with Shibuya's crossing chaos, retro games in Akihabara, and temples tucked into Asakusa’s alleys.

— Business Events Tokyo

Paris

Paris enchants with the Eiffel Tower’s twinkle, glory at Notre Dame Cathedral, and of the Louvre.

— My French Country Home Magazine

Rome

Rome is a living museum of ancient ruins like the Colosseum, Vatican art treasures, and wishes cast at Trevi Fountain.

— Vox City

Singapore

Singapore impresses with Marina Bay’s skyline, futuristic Gardens by the Bay, and the fun in Sentosa.

— ART:SG

Thailand

Thailand is filled with golden temples in Bangkok, elephant sanctuaries in Chiang Mai, and beach bliss in Phuket.

— Asia Odyssey Travel

Hawaii

Hawaii impresses with surfing in Oahu, volcano hikes on Big Island, and scenic drives across Maui’s coast.

— Tripster

New York

New York City thrills with Times Square lights, Central Park strolls, Broadway shows, and iconic skylines.

— CityPASS
